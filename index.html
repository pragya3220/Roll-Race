RULES:
  • Whenever a player gets 6 points after rolling the dice, they'll get another dice roll.
  • A player will only be able to move their piece when they get 6 and unlock their piece.
  • Whenever a player ends their move over another player's piece, that piece will return to its initial position, and the first player will get an extra dice roll.
  • Whenever a player makes it home, they'll be given an extra dice roll.
